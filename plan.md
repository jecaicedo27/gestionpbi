# Add Carriots Summary to EMPAQUE Selector

## Change
- Add a new "Resumen de Carritos" (Delivery Summary) block to the top of the `EMPAQUE` selector module in `IntroStep.jsx`.
- The block will pull `carriots` from the `CONTEO` processParameters and render them elegantly, showing the product name, the quantity, and the dispatch/reception photos side-by-side.
- This will satisfy the user's request for a clear visualization of the production deliveries (carritos) independent of which specific Empaque note they click on.
